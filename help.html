Help

Quick instructions for candidates and employers
Quick start
  • Candidates: browse jobs on the main Job Board, then log in to apply.
  • Employers: use Employer Portal to manage postings and applicants.
  • If you get signed out, just log in again — your session is stored in your browser.
How to search and filter jobs
  • Use the keyword search at the top to filter by title/description keywords.
  • Use filters for category, work arrangement, city, and state/territory.
  • “Use My Location” can prefill your city/state and optionally apply a radius filter.
How to create an account / log in
  • Click Login / Sign Up on the Job Board.
  • Choose Candidate (Job Seeker) if you’re applying for jobs.
  • After you sign in, you can access your dashboard under My Account.
How to apply
  • Select a job from the list to view details.
  • Click Apply.
  • If you aren’t logged in, you’ll be taken to login first, then returned to continue.
How to report a job
  • Select a job and click Report Job.
  • Choose a reason and write a short message with what’s wrong.
  • Reports are reviewed by our team.
Candidate Dashboard
  • Go to My Account → Dashboard to see your applications.
  • You’ll see a visual progress tracker for each application (Applied → Reviewed → Interview → Offer → Accepted/Rejected).
